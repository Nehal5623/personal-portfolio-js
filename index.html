About Me

Hello! I'm Nehal Rani Sinha, a 20-year-old student currently in my 3rd year of pursuing a BTech degree at the prestigious IIT Roorkee. I'm passionate about technology, and my journey has led me to become a full-stack web developer. Beyond academics, I'm an active member of various campus activities and organizations.

One of my roles that I take immense pride in is being the Marketing Manager at Team KNOx, a prominent group here at IIT Roorkee. It's a position that has honed my leadership and communication skills, allowing me to contribute effectively to team dynamics.
My enthusiasm for engineering goes beyond the classroom, as I've had the incredible opportunity to represent IIT Roorkee in the National Competition of SAE BAJA. This experience has not only expanded my technical knowledge but has also provided me with valuable teamwork and problem-solving skills.

On the technical front, I possess a strong command of programming languages like C++ and Python, which have been essential tools in my journey. I've honed my web development skills, working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I'm always eager to explore new technologies and stay up-to-date with the latest trends in the tech industry.
Hackathons have been a thrilling part of my journey, allowing me to put my problem-solving skills to the test and collaborate with like-minded individuals. I'm an active member of various coding platforms like CodeChef and Codeforces, where I continually challenge myself to enhance my coding prowess.
